Welcome Guests

Whether you are celebrating your honeymoon, vacation or simply thinking about relaxing at a place that will captivate your mind with its exotic ambiance, Travellers Beach Resort is the ideal getaway to unwind and enjoy paradise in Hagnaya on the exquisite island of Cebu.

Located on Bantayan's famous white, sandy Seven-Mile Beach caressed by the warmth of the sun, this family owned Hagnaya beach resort offers several room types for charismatic relaxation, entertainment, and a contented living space that exemplifies the finest services a resort has to offer.

White Sand Palace Resort was founded in 1992 by Winston Wellington. Mr. Wellington, quite the traveler himself, began Travellers as a bar and restaurant with a single bungalow. The land; originally wild bantayan bush, was cleared by Wellington himself, leaving three beautiful sea grape trees that still offer shade to sunbathers and restaurant guests on the beach. Having started from humble beginnings, Travellers has grown tremendously to 60 rooms including a vast array of resort accommodations. Conveniently situated a 5 minute walk from the Bantayan town centre, Travellers prime location offers those who wish an opportunity to explore Negril to truly get an understanding of Cebu's rich culture and heritage.
