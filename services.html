Amenities


1. Free Wi-Fi

Free Wi-Fi is available in the resort whether you're in the room, corrighdor, Lounge, and restaurant.



2. Restaurant

The restaurant is located at the ground floor, beside the counters. Some meals are served free as a part of the plan rate. The restaurant accepts major credit cards and debit cards for additional orders not covered by the rates.

 

3. Swimming Pool

A swimming pool is also available for all guests and customers, though there are safety guidelines you must strictly follow. It is good for those opting out to swim in the sea, they still have a swimming pool to enjoy.



4. Motor Boat

Motor boats are available in coast of the resort, it is not owned by the resort but by the locals of Bantayan, but it is available for those who desire to go Island hopping.

Price Rate: 2500PHP-4000PHP

 

5. Basketball Court

A basketball court is also available in the resort. It's completely free for the customers. You have to borrow basketballs in the counter if you don't have one.
