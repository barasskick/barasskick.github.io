Room Information


1. Single Bed Premium

Single Bed Premium room is a spacious room good for one to two occupants. It is affordable and it offers all amenities for free except the motor boats.

Dimensions: 3X4

Rates: 6000PHP per night



2. Double Bed Premium

Double Bed Premium is a good option for a big family going for a budget rate, the rooms look luxurious and has its own small terrace, it is good for three to 6 occupants. All amenities are offered free except motor boats.

Dimensions: 3X6

Rates: 9000PHP per night

 

3. Master Bedroom

If you have a big family, and you decided that you'll separate the children's room and yours, the Master bedroom is the best to avail. it is just beside every Double Bed Premium room to serve its purposes. All amenities are offered free including coverages in motor boats.

Dimensions: 4X5

Rates: 11KPHP if availing the Double bed premium, 10KPHP if not



4. Triple Bed Premium

For group vacations, Triple Bed Premium room can accomodate up to 12 occupants. All amenities are offered free including coverages in motor boats.

Dimensions: 4X7

Rates: 15KPHP

 

5. Honeymoon Suite

For the newly weds, Honeymoon suite is available for such a sweet rate, enjoy the ambiance of your unity as husband and wife and enjoy the suite.All amenities are offered free including coverages in motor boats.

Dimensions: 5X5

Rates: 16KPHP
